About me

As a passionate Post-doctoral fellow in CSIRO's Data61 (PhD in Computer Science and Engineering, University of New South Wales (UNSW), Australia), I am eager to pursue my industrial research and development aspirations. My goal is to create socially beneficial products that positively impact many lives. My research expertise includes User-Centric Privacy, Large Language Models, Data Privacy, Quantification of Privacy, Artificial Intelligence, Differential Privacy, Natural Language Processing, and Statistical Analysis. I am committed to exploring and adopting new technologies and continuously updating my knowledge to stay at the forefront of the field.
